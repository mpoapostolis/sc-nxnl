## Full description (max 4000 characters)

```
Skin Coach is your pocket skincare companion. Take a selfie and get an instant
Skin Score, a breakdown of what your skin needs, and a personalised routine —
in seconds, right on your phone.

Meet Lumi, your friendly skin coach. She reads your selfie, cheers you on, and
helps you turn skincare into a daily habit that actually sticks.

WHAT YOU GET
• Skin Score — a simple 0–100 read on your skin, tracked over time
• Concern breakdown — acne, fine lines, pores, dark spots, redness, oiliness
• A personalised morning & evening routine, built from your results
• Daily streak — check off your routine each day and watch your streak grow
• Progress tracking — see your Skin Score trend across every scan
• Friendly coach tips from Lumi, in plain language

100% PRIVATE
Your photos never leave your phone. The face detection, the analysis and your
history all run entirely on your device. No upload, no cloud, no account, no
sign-up. Delete any scan whenever you like — uninstalling removes everything.

FREE
Skin Coach is completely free. No subscription, no paywall.

A GENTLE NOTE
Skin Coach is an at-a-glance wellness check to help you build good habits — it
is not a medical device and does not provide a diagnosis. For any real skin
concern, please see a dermatologist.
```
