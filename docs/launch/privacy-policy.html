Skin Coach — Privacy Policy

Effective 19 May 2026

The short version: Skin Coach does not collect your data. Your photos and results never leave your phone — there is no cloud, no account, and nothing is uploaded anywhere.

What Skin Coach accesses

Skin Coach uses your device camera so you can take a selfie for a skin scan. The camera is only used while you are actively taking a scan.

How your photo is used

When you take a scan, the photo is analysed entirely on your device to produce your Skin Score, a breakdown of skin concerns, and a suggested routine. Face detection and analysis run locally on your phone. The photo is never sent to us or to any third party.

What is stored, and where

Your scans — a cropped face photo and the resulting scores — are saved only in Skin Coach's private storage on your own device, so you can see your progress over time. This data stays on your phone.

What Skin Coach does NOT do

  • It does not upload your photos or data to any server.
  • It does not require an account or sign-up.
  • It does not use analytics or advertising trackers.
  • It does not sell or share your data with anyone — there is nothing to share.

Your control

You can delete any individual scan from within the app at any time. Uninstalling Skin Coach permanently removes all of its data from your device.

Children

Skin Coach is a general-audience skincare app and is not directed at children under 13.

A note on what Skin Coach is

Skin Coach offers an at-a-glance wellness check to help you build good skincare habits. It is not a medical device and does not provide a medical diagnosis. For any real skin concern, please consult a dermatologist.

Changes to this policy
